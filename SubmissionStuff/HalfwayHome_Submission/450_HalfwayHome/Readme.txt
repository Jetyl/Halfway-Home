TEAM: Makeshift Monocle
GAME: Halfway Home
ENGINE: Unity

GAMEPLAY: Gameplay is divided into two spaces.
In Scenes: Click with the mouse to progress forward in the narrative. When presented with a choice, 
	simply click on the button corresponding to your desired outcome. Other buttons in the space offer
	additional functionality and will teach their corresponding keyboard shortcuts on mouseover.
On the Map: Select and confirm where in the home you wish to spend time. You will experience a
	scene specific to that location that will modify your stats. Sometimes you will witness a special scene.
