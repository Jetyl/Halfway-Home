TEAM: Makeshift Monocule
GAME: Halfway Home
ENGINE: Unity

GAMEPLAY: Gameplay is divided between dialog mode, and map mode.
		In Dialog Mode: click with the mouse, to progress forward in the narrative. when a choice pops up, 
				move mous and click to make that choice. 
		In Map Mode: Select, and confirm where in the home you wish to spend time. depending on where you
				pick, a plot scene will be discovered, or not.
